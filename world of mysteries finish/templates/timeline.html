Every mystery begins somewhere in the forgotten past.

The Ancient World

The Seven Kingdoms were not always enemies. Long before the academy era, the world was shaped by Aura, sea disasters, and hidden wars.

Ancient History

2000 Years Ago — Origin of Sea Gates

The first Sea Gates appeared from the ocean depths. Monsters entered the world, and warriors began awakening Aura to survive.

Ancient Era — Birth of Seven Kingdoms

Humanity divided into seven kingdoms based on elemental Aura powers: Fire, Water, Wind, Lightning, Earth, Dark, and Ice.

50 Years Ago — Seven Nation War

A massive war changed the balance of the world. Many truths were erased from history, and the official records became controlled by the ruling powers.

Present Era — Warrior Academy of Sea

Young warriors are trained to fight Sea Gate monsters. Kai, Jinga, and Tyson begin their journey during this era.

Future Mystery — The Truth of Fire Kingdom

Kai does not know the truth of his bloodline in the beginning. The mystery of the Fire Kingdom will slowly reveal the hidden history of the world.
