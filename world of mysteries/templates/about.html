About World of Mysteries

An original fantasy anime universe created with passion, worldbuilding, mystery, and adventure.

About the Project

World of Mysteries is an original anime-inspired fantasy universe created by I.M.Kumar. The story combines adventure, friendship, rivalry, hidden history, academy life, elemental powers, and deep world mysteries.

The universe follows Kai Flameson, Jinga Flameson, Tyson Hattari, and many other warriors as they uncover the truth behind the Seven Kingdoms, Sea Gates, Aura power, and ancient wars.

Creative Inspiration

The project is inspired by the feeling of large-scale anime adventures, powerful rivalries, emotional friendships, mysterious worlds, and fantasy kingdoms. However, the characters, world rules, Sea Gates, and Aura system are part of the original World of Mysteries universe.

Project Goal

The goal is to slowly build World of Mysteries into a complete story universe through web chapters, character profiles, kingdom lore, and future manga-style content.
