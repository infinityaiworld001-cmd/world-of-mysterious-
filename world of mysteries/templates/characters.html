Characters

Meet the young warriors, rivals, teachers, and powerful students who shape the beginning of World of Mysteries.

FIRST GENERATION

Main Characters

These characters begin the journey inside the Warrior Academy of Sea.

Kai Flameson

Kai is a calm and serious fire aura user. He does not know the full truth of his bloodline in the beginning, but he carries mysterious fire energy connected to the destroyed Fire Kingdom.

Age: 16 Fire Aura Silent Hero

Jinga Flameson

Jinga is Kai's cheerful younger brother. He is funny, emotional, and hungry most of the time, but he has hidden Ice Aura potential that may awaken as the story grows.

Age: 15 Ice Aura Comedy Heart

Tyson Hattari

Tyson is a skilled warrior from the Wind Kingdom. He first becomes suspicious of Kai's fire aura and later becomes both rival and ally during academy life.

Age: 16 Wind Aura Rival Ally
ACADEMY POWERHOUSES

Elite Characters

Strong warriors and mysterious figures who influence the academy arc.

Eresawa

Eresawa is a legendary teacher at the Warrior Academy of Sea. Calm, dangerous, and mysterious, he has the ability to suppress or erase aura during training.

Teacher Mystery Aura Legendary

Free Dela

Free Dela is considered one of the strongest students in the academy. He is hardworking, silent, and carries an intimidating warrior presence among the first-year students.

Elite Student Hard Work Strongest Presence

Lui

Lui is a dangerous and mysterious student with blue flame energy. His aura feels unstable, powerful, and frightening, making him one of the most feared figures in the academy.

Blue Flame Dangerous Mysterious

Character Theme

World of Mysteries focuses on friendship, rivalry, hidden identity, academy growth, ancient war secrets, and the emotional journey of young warriors. Each character is designed to carry a unique personality, aura type, and future role in the larger mystery of the Seven Kingdoms.
