Sea Gates

Ancient ocean portals that release monsters into the world and hide the greatest mystery of history.

The Ocean Opens

Sea Gates appear near oceans, lakes, and deep water zones. When the water level rises, the monsters become stronger and more dangerous.

DANGER SYSTEM

Sea Gate Levels

Each gate has a different threat level depending on water energy and monster pressure.

Level 1 Gate

Small monster waves. New academy students can survive with teamwork.

Level 2 Gate

Stronger creatures appear. Students need proper aura control to fight back.

Level 3 Gate

Large monsters and unstable water energy. Elite warriors are required.

Black Gate

A forbidden gate connected to disasters, missing warriors, and ancient secrets.

Ancient Gate

A legendary gate said to be linked to the beginning of Aura itself.

Unknown Gate

A mysterious gate that does not follow normal aura rules or kingdom records.

Sea Gate Mystery

The official history says Sea Gates are natural disasters. But some warriors believe the gates were created during an ancient war.

Kai, Jinga, and Tyson first encounter a Sea Gate near a lake. That incident becomes the beginning of their journey toward the Warrior Academy of Sea.
