Episode 1: New Beginning Summary

In a world ruled by Seven Kingdoms, Kai and Jinga travel through a forest. Jinga is hungry and cheerful, while Kai remains silent and serious. Tyson appears and notices Kai's strange fire aura.

At a lake, a Sea Gate opens and monsters attack. Kai tries to use fire aura, but his power is unstable. Tyson saves Jinga using wind aura and defeats the monsters. After this incident, the three boys head toward the Warrior Academy of Sea.
