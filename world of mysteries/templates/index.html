A World Ruled by Aura

In this world, power is not only strength. It is kingdom, bloodline, history, mystery, and destiny.

Story Introduction

The World of Mysteries is a fantasy anime universe where seven kingdoms are built around elemental Aura powers. Royals control power while common people struggle to survive. From the ocean depths, Sea Gates release monsters into the world, forcing young warriors to train and fight for humanity's survival.

Kai Flameson is a silent boy carrying fire aura without understanding the full truth of his past. Alongside his cheerful brother Jinga and the confident wind warrior Tyson, Kai enters the Warrior Academy of Sea, where destiny, rivalry, friendship, and ancient secrets begin to unfold.

Episode 1 Summary

Kai and Jinga travel through a forest while struggling with hunger and uncertainty. Tyson appears and becomes suspicious of Kai's fire aura. At a lake, a Sea Gate suddenly opens and monsters attack. Tyson saves Jinga using wind aura, while Kai realizes his fire power is still unstable. After the incident, the trio moves toward the Warrior Academy of Sea, where their destiny begins.
