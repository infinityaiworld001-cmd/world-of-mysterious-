The Seven Kingdoms

The world is divided into seven powerful kingdoms, each connected to a unique Aura element and ancient history.

🔥 Fire Kingdom

A kingdom believed to have been destroyed many years ago. Hidden secrets connect Kai Flameson to its forgotten legacy.

🌊 Water Kingdom

A powerful maritime nation controlling sea trade routes, ports, and ocean defense forces.

🌪 Wind Kingdom

Home of Tyson Hattari. Famous for speed, agility, strategy, and skilled warriors.

⚡ Lightning Kingdom

The current ruling kingdom that dominates the world. Its leaders claim to protect peace and order.

🌎 Earth Kingdom

Known for strong defenses, giant fortresses, and warriors with unmatched endurance.

🌑 Dark Kingdom

A mysterious nation connected to forbidden knowledge, hidden experiments, and lost history.

❄ Ice Kingdom

A cold northern kingdom filled with ancient traditions and powerful ice aura users.

Kingdom History

Long ago, the Seven Kingdoms lived in balance. However, wars, betrayals, and the appearance of Sea Gates changed the world forever.

Today, many secrets have been hidden from history. The younger generation believes the official stories, but the truth may be completely different.
