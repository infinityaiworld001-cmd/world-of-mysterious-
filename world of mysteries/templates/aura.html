Aura System

Aura is the source of power in the World of Mysteries. Every warrior is born with the potential to awaken an Aura.

🔥 Fire Aura

Focused on destruction, power, courage, and explosive attacks.

🌊 Water Aura

Balanced power that can heal, defend, and adapt to many situations.

🌪 Wind Aura

Grants speed, movement, aerial attacks, and mobility.

⚡ Lightning Aura

One of the fastest aura types, specializing in rapid attacks.

🌎 Earth Aura

Strong defense, durability, and immense physical strength.

🌑 Dark Aura

A dangerous aura connected to shadows, secrets, and forbidden powers.

❄ Ice Aura

Uses freezing attacks, control, and battlefield manipulation.

✨ Mythical Aura

The rarest aura type in existence. Only a few individuals possess it.

How Aura Works

Aura is awakened through training, emotion, combat, and natural talent.

Most warriors spend years mastering a single Aura type. However, some rare individuals possess abilities beyond the understanding of ordinary people.

Legends speak of warriors capable of changing history using Mythical Aura.
