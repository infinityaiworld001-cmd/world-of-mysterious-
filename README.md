# 🌍 World of Mysteries

World of Mysteries is an original fantasy anime-inspired universe created by I.M.Kumar.

The project combines adventure, friendship, rivalry, academy life, elemental powers, hidden history, and world mysteries into a unique story experience.

## 🔥 Story Overview

The world is divided into Seven Kingdoms, each connected to a powerful Aura element.

Ancient Sea Gates continue to appear across the world, releasing dangerous monsters and threatening humanity.

Kai Flameson, Jinga Flameson, and Tyson Hattari begin their journey at the Warrior Academy of Sea, where they discover secrets that could change the world forever.
